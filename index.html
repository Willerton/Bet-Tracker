Data Management

Your data is stored automatically in your browser on this device using localStorage. Export a backup whenever you want a copy you can move elsewhere.

How storage works

Data is saved automatically in your browser. You do not need to export every time just to keep your own data on the same browser and same site URL. Export is mainly for backup, moving to another browser/device, or restoring after clearing data.
